My Project

Wet & Dry Waste Sorting Machine – Sorttech is a wet & dry waste sorting machine designed to help people separate waste correctly with less effort. It uses sensors to detect if the waste is wet or dry and automatically sorts it into the proper bin.

Diorama Project – This diorama shows a smart city that uses solar panels and wind turbines for clean energy. It includes important buildings like a hospital, school, and police station to support the community.

Skin Care Brochure – This skincare brochure provides essential tips and product information to help you achieve healthy, glowing skin through a simple daily routine.

Mind Glow Gummies – Personalized vitamin gummies that help students and young adults reduce stress, sleep, boost energy.

Counting Sort vs. Bucket Sort – This research compares Bucket Sort, which distributes data into groups, and Counting Sort, which calculates the frequency of values to organize information efficiently.
